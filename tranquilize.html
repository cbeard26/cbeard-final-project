The Story


This was the first of the two songs that I worked on, so I was still
in the process of discovering where I wanted to go with my project.
I started out by trying to replicate the sound of the original, but
quickly found that it was difficult to emulate the sounds of live
instruments using the samplers and MIDI instruments that are available
in Logic Pro; I decided that, rather than spend too much time
fussing over small details, I would try to replicate the feeling of
the original while embracing the unique sound that the use of MIDI
instruments creates. In that respect, I think I was successful, but
I also learned a lot about the process of creating this type of music
during the process. The first instrument that I tried to write in was
the guitar, which proved to be very inefficient. After a bit of
frustration, I restarted from the bottom up, creating the drums and
bass first, then the higher instruments, then the vocals. This method
was much more effective for me: getting the structure in place allowed
me to more easily see where I wanted the piece to be by the end.
