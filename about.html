About

I decided to use this open ended project opportunity to take
a deeper dive into the production of one type of music that I
really enjoy: Rock. In the end, it sort of became a case study
of the band Finish Ticket, whose music I particularly enjoy.
What I find so enjoyable about their music is the variety of
textures that they're able to create using a limited arsenal of
musical instruments. Their ability to morph sounds in such a way
as to encapsulate a feeling or emotion never ceases to amaze me.
I've recently been reexploring their music, and thought that I'd
try to reconstruct – or reimagine – some of my favorites. I worked
entirely in Logic Pro to make this music, mainly becasue I do not
play any of the instruments that the band uses at any considerable
level of proficiency, and Logic's variety of built in sounds at
least gave me a place to start. Taking on a larger project was a
fun experience in many ways; I learned about time-management methods
that I could apply in order to streamline my work, and developed
musical problem solving skills when faced with a sound I couldn't
easily replicate.
