The Story


This song was completed on a bit of a time-crunch, especially the
vocals, but it was a much more efficient process because of the
knowledge that I gained while making Tranquilize. Starting with the
percussion was very helpful in this song because the pre-chorus breaks
from typical timing schemes very subtly. Laying out the timing of
everything streamlined the production process, allowing me to get a
substantial amount of work done in a relatively short time. As with
Tranquilize, I worked from the bottom to the top, first creating the
drums and bass, then the guitars and pads, then the vocals. I took
more creative liberties with this song, especially with the way that
the soundscape manifests itself in the chorus; replicating the guitar
quility of that section proved near impossible for me, so I composed a
guitar piece that I believe captures the same feeling, even if it doesn't
make the same use of distortion and pitch-bending found in the original.
I am happy with the way that this has turned out, especially considering
the narrow timeline.
